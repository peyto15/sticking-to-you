About Sticking with You

Hello! I’m the creator behind Sticking with You — a brand dedicated to spreading joy through the cutest stickers you’ll ever meet. Our mascot is a cheerful capybara, often spotted with a mandarin orange perched on its head, symbolizing warmth, calm, and a splash of fun.

Every sticker is designed with love, inspired by nature, animals, and the little moments that make us smile. Whether you’re decorating your laptop, water bottle, or journal, our stickers are here to brighten your day.

Thank you for supporting my small business and joining me in this capybara adventure!
